Introduction

Welcome to the portal Studio where you can create own design for virtual clothing.
We use a tool called spline for creating our clothes, and you can use this tool as well on our website.

What is spline

Spline is a tool for creating 3D designs and gives the option to use preset of clothes in their free open library.
You can customize these clothes with images or patterns you want, so let your creative go wild!

How to use it

When u open the tool you will need to register to spline to use it first
It's the easiest to pick one of their pre-made clothing peaces to continue your design
After you got your 3D design open you can change the image on the material section to anything you like.
Once you are satisfied you can export under type Web content or public url
You will see it generates a URL and a Iframe you will need to copy the URL and enter it into the section to upload your design
