About Us

STOCK

Portal Wardrobe is meant to stock all your virtual belongings in one place. Here you can organise all the clothing you own.

INSPIRE

In the Portal Hub you can inspire others by sharing all the items you own, but also the ones you have created. You can even upload pictures wearing your clothing to inspire others.

CREATE

One of our main functions consists of being able to create your own virtual clothes. This is all possible with the Portal Studio feature.

EXPRESS

With all the clothing you own, you are able to express yourself in the online world. With our function to export your virtuel goods as AR filters, you are able to wear whatever you own, wherever you want to.
